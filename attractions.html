What To Do When You Get Get Here

Local attractions

Hooligan Village - Houses plenty of the locals, adorned with dragon motifs. All the houses have a unique setting as they sit on the side of a mountain.

The Great Hall - This is the central hub of the island. Villagers use the Great Hall to eat, socialise and have village meeting. Sit down enjoy a drink or a few and then have a punch-up. (Included free of charge.)

Barn - Enjoy the sights and smells of a barn that houses yaks, chicken and sheep.

Armoury - Houses the weapons and gunpowder.

Docks - The docks are based at the bottom of Raven's Point. Walk along an authentic wooden walkway looking out over the harbour showcasing all the viking ships when berthed. As well as looking over the two magnificent viking statues breathing fire that guard the Berk Docks.

Cemetery - Visit the fallen that are buried in this wooden marvel and gape at the Cryptic Collector (statue) standing over and protecting them.

Haddock House - This is the chiefs house, situated at the top of the highest hill looking over the entire village and located next to the Great Hall. Be careful, the chief is an almighty man and can fight.

Blacksmith Forge - Opposite the chiefs house is Gobber's Blacksmithing Forge that houses all the weapons Viking use on a day to day basis.Enjoy playing with swords, watching how they made. Maybe Gobber will let you have a go at forging steel.

Raven's Point - At the base of this fine point sits the docks and at the top there are plenty of forests. Watch yourself anything can be lurking in these woods.

The Cove - in the forest there is an area that has sunk and can be reached by navigating through narrow corriders in the steep walls. Once you are through you are greeted by a large body of water with an abundance of fish and a waterfall cascading. mind the large boulders and steep walls.

Gothi's Hut - Here is where the village elder 'Gothi' lives. As you will see the hut is built on scaffolding and has many steps with an excellent view of the entire village.

Berk Training Academy - Once used to train all future vikings in the ways of defeating dragons. It houses several impressive cages along the edge of the arena all are equiped with superior locking. Nothing is getting out of these impressive heavyduty wooden doors.
